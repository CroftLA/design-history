A design history is a public way to document changes to a service. It tells the story of what design changes have be made and why.

You can publish design histories using our CMS, which is maintained and supported by DesignOps.

Request access to CMS

Before you start a design history, you need to request access to the Design History CMS service where you will write and publish content.

Access can only be provided to people with an education.gov.uk email address.

Send a request and DesignOps will contact you within 2 working days with details to sign in to the CMS.

Guidance for using the CMS

We have put together step by step guidance for starting a design history, managing images and media and writing posts.
