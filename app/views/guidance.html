{% endblock %} {% block content %} {% markdown %} The following guidance will help you to get a design history up and running for your team or service.
We use a content management system (CMS) for design histories published on this service. We aim to set you up within 1 working day.
- [Request access to the CMS](#request-access-to-the-cms) - [Set up your team page](#set-up-your-team-page) - [Set up your service](#set-up-your-service) - [Write your first post](#write-your-first-post) - [Additional guidance and support](#additional-guidance-and-support)
## Request access to the CMS [Submit an access request](//start-design-history) to use the CMS. You'll need to provide your name and email address. Only requests for access from education.gov.uk and digital.education.gov.uk addresses will be accepted. We will generate a random password. You can change this to something more memorable in the CMS yourself, [find out how to change your password](#change-your-password). We aim to set up all users within 1 working day. Usually in a few hours. ## Set up your team page
Teams are used to group multiple services together.
Sign into the CMS. From the side navigation menu, select **Content Manager** and then choose **Team**. If your team doesn't exist, you can create a new one. Click the button named **Create new entry**. On the create an entry page, complete the **Title** of the team, give it a **Description**. When you are happy with the title, in the **Slug** field, click the Regenerate icon to ensure the slug is generated. Click the **Save** button. Your team will remain in draft until you click the **Publish** button. ## Set up your service
Services are used to group posts together. Multiple services can exist under a Team.
Sign into the CMS. From the side navigation menu, select **Content Manager** and then choose **Service**. If your service doesn't exist, you can create a new one. Click the button named **Create new entry**. On the create an entry page, complete the **Title** of the service, give it a **Description**. When you are happy with the title, in the **Slug** field, click the Regenerate icon to ensure the slug is generated. If your service and posts under the service need to be protected with a password, enter one into the **Password** field. There are no restrictions on what this can be. If a password is set, any service, or post associated with the service, will require a password to access. Click the **Save** button. Your service will remain in draft until you click the **Publish** button. ## Create your media library folder Sign into the CMS. From the side navigation menu, select **Media Library** If your service does not have a folder, click the **Add new folder** button and name your folder the same as your service You can then save all images relating to your service and posts into this folder. Additionally, you can have child folders in your service folder for each post - It's up to you if you if you do this, but it's recommended. ## Write your first post
Posts are grouped by service.
Sign into the CMS. From the side navigation menu, select **Content Manager** and then choose **Post**. If your post doesn't exist, you can create a new one. Click the button named **Create new entry**. On the create an entry page, complete the **Title** of the post, type a **Lede**, (this is used for the meta description and introduction to the post). Type our your post in the **Body** field using Markdown. When you are happy with the title, in the **Slug** field, click the Regenerate icon to ensure the slug is generated. Select a **Publication date**, this can be any date. Click the **Save** button. Your post will remain in draft until you click the **Publish** button.
## Additional guidance and support ### Change your password Passwords must contain 1 uppercase letter, 1 or more numbers and be longer than 8 characters in total. If you want to change your password, sign into the CMS using your email address and current password. On the bottom left of the admin screen, where your name is displayed, click this and choose Profile. On the profile page, you need to enter your current password, and your new password, and confirm your new password. Click the save button. ### If you can't remember your password Do not use the "Reset your password" link on the sign in page, we have disabled this functionality. Send an email to [design.ops@education.gov.uk](mailto:design.ops@education.gov.uk) and we will reset your password. {% endmarkdown %} {% endblock %} {% block sideContent %}{% endblock %}
